Features

Dynamic Quiz Selection

Offers a variety of quiz categories and topics for users to choose from. Each category provides a unique set of questions, ensuring diverse and engaging quiz experiences.

Customizable Quiz Settings

Allows users to customize their quiz experience by adjusting parameters like the number of questions or difficulty level. This feature enables tailored quizzes suited to individual preferences.

Personalized Quiz History

Keeps track of users' quiz performance and history. Users can view their past quiz results, revisit quizzes they've completed, and track their progress over time.
